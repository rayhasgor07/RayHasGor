VOLUNTEERING

From July to August 2024 I was enrolled in this program in which we learned how to organize our own event and I created a fundrasing event for Palestine. I booked a venue, I contacted the cateering and I also made a budget sheet to see our revenue.
AL FALAH ISLAMIC CENTRE (60 hrs) During Ramadan in both 2023 and 2024, I volunteered my time and efforts at the mosque, engaging in various activities to support the community. This included distributing water bottles and dates to attendees, providing boxes of food after prayers, organizing logistics, and undertaking tasks as needed. My contributions aimed to facilitate a smooth and meaningful experience during Ramadan for all participants.
ICNA FEST (15 hrs) In August 2023 and 2024, I had the opportunity to participate in ICNA Fest where I eagerly volunteered my time to assist in setting up tents, bouncy castles, and chairs. Throughout the event, I also took on the responsibility of monitoring the guests, including the children.
540 GOLDEN HAWKS AIR CADET SQUADRON (10 hrs) In November 2023, I actively participated in a fundraising initiative for Remembrance Day, where I successfully raised approximately $200-300 CAD.
